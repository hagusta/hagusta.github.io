The Loss Explosion

15 Nov 2017 A common problem during deep network training is diminished or exploded loss. During cs224d assigment 3 training I encoutered exploding loss problem. I employ few tricks that I found in the web to counter this problem: weight initialization, Layer normalization and optimization. I am not sure which trick is more important. More research is needed
